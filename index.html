Tacos and Tequila

About Magnolia's

At Magnolia’s our mission is to provide the world with exceptional and unique tacos, delicious hand crafted margaritas and tequila inspired cocktails, and a fun knowledgeable experience with various types of tequila and mezcal. Our menu looks to combine the traditional flavors and spices of Mexico with innovative and contemporary combinations. Our location in the heart of downtown Harrisonburg boasts 3 levels of dining, including a panoramic view rooftop experience and pet-friendly patio ground level. Magnolia’s is the perfect place to find some unique tacos, great libations, and a rustic but modern atmosphere.

Our History

Magnolia’s is inspired by the traditional cooking and flavors from Jalisco, Mexico. At Magnolia’s we strive to provide you the freshest, most delicious and unparalleled tacos. We use the freshest, quality ingredients that are prepared in house daily. Our tequila selection features over 100 different tequilas and mezcals. We also feature an extensive beer & wine selection with Domestic and Mexican beers, and your favorite styles of wine like Merlot, Pinot Grigio, Cabernet Sauvignon, and more.

Menu

Indulge in our delicious and authentic menu, showcasing all your favorite Mexican classics, including our signature tacos. Complement your meal with our wide selection of specialty drinks, featuring ice-cold beer, premium liquors, and handcrafted in-house cocktails made fresh just for you.
